The Journey

From Hartford, CT to the global stage — a life driven by curiosity and the desire to teach.

1985 — Early Life
Hartford, Connecticut
Born to a Guatemalan mother and Puerto Rican father. Attended Howell Cheney Technical High School studying Microcomputer. Early coding with GRAAL scripts and RPG Maker.
2003–2007 — College
Bowling Green State University
Studied Marketing & Popular Culture. Promotion Director at WBGU-FM. Founded Fashionably Numb Music and The Gamers Lounge.
2008–2019 — NYC
Financial Education & Public Service
Trainer at Greico Financial Training. Ran for public office 3 times. Vice Chair of the LNC (2018-2020).
2019 — Transition
Software Development
Created GrokOverflow.com. Instructor at General Assembly. Full-stack development at Crossfield Digital.
2021–Present
